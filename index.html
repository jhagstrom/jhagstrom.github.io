Hey there

So happy to welcome you here. Let me give you a brief tour of how to get around here.

This is the starting page. From here you can scroll down just a little bit to book a coaching session with me, or scroll down some more to find a few featured articles of mine.

The navigation is really simple, up top is the navigation bar. Clicking on the Laophorum logo will bring you back here everytime. Click on Coaching and you will arrive at a page where I explain the different services I offer in more detail. Clicking Blog brings you to a page where you can get to all my articles. Not just the featured ones. About is where you want to click if you want to learn more about me and this site. The looking glass brings you to a search page, where you can choose to either use Bing or Google to search for content keywords on Laophorum.

Right here, immediately below, you can choose to follow me on Bluesky, or subscribe to my newsletter on Substack. Why not do both?
